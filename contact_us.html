Nairobi Camp

Nairobi’s Camp is a tented camp that combines tradition with comfort and it’s a model of understated, intimate luxury. Situated in the southern, wilder corner of Kenya’s mighty Nairobi National Park reserve close to the confluence of the city – an idyllic geographical feature that is close to the capital.

Maasai Mara Camp

Mara’s Camp location is a big draw, close to Tanzania with wonderful views of the Serengeti National Park, it is one of the first camps in the Mara to witness the legendary annual migration of wildebeest, which thunders by from June to September each year. Mara’s Camp also offers the best of the Masai Mara’s wildlife and birdlife year round.
