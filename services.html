Activity Services

If you prefer a more experienced local tourist guide to plan your vacation activities, we recommend you to choose one of our 3- or 5-day activity packages. We will fill your vacation with the right amount of relaxation, sport activities and adventures, and take care of all the transportation, tickets and equipment that you will need. Naturally, we can adapt the programmes to your wishes – with regard to the contents of the programme, time or the level of difficulty. Please send us your wishes on the following address: info@nairobicamp.
